Swimming

Dogs can benefit from swimming as it provides an excellent low-impact form of exercise that helps maintain their cardiovascular health and muscle strength while reducing stress on joints. Additionally, swimming can be a fun and refreshing activity, especially in hot weather, promoting both physical and mental well-being in dogs.

Treadmill Exercise

Dogs should run regularly as it is a natural and instinctual behavior that helps them expend energy, maintain a healthy weight, and promote cardiovascular health. Running also stimulates their mind, enhances overall well-being, and fosters a strong bond with their owners during shared exercise activities.

Lift Bro

Dogs can benefit from weightlifting exercises when performed under proper guidance and supervision. Weightlifting helps to build muscle strength, improve overall fitness, and promote joint health in dogs, contributing to their overall physical well-being and enhancing athletic performance in certain dog sports and activities. However, it is essential to consult with a veterinarian to ensure that your dog isn't a weakling that'll buckle under the pressure. Get swole nerd.
